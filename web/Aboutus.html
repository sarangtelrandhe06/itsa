About Us

Welcome to the Information Technology Student Association (ITSA)!

Established in 2024, ITSA is a student-led committee dedicated to promoting innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence among IT students.

Our goal is to create a platform where students can learn, collaborate, and grow through workshops, coding events, and community-driven projects.
